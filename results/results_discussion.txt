Results Discussion
- Features: TextBlob sentence-level polarity only (1D feature).
- Model: MLP trained from scratch with manual forward pass, backprop, and gradient descent.
- Strengths: Works when reviews have clearly positive/negative polarity.
- Errors: Sarcasm and mixed reviews are hard when using only one polarity feature.
- Improvement (if allowed): Hybrid (VADER + TextBlob polarity) or more features (not allowed here).
